Step into the year 2030, a time when reality and nightmares intertwine.

In this dystopian world, the Carnival stands as a testament to humanity's fascination with the macabre. It's a place where the boundary between thrill and terror blurs, and visitors willingly surrender to the dark unknown. Each attraction, each performer, and each corner of the carnival is a doorway to a different kind of nightmare.

As a daring visitor, you'll embark on a journey that blurs the line between thrill-seeker and thrill-maker. Can you withstand the chilling allure of the Crimson Carnival, or will you succumb to its sinister charms? The choice is yours.

FEATURES:

EXPLORE A TWISTED REALITY — The year 2030 is a world turned upside down by technological marvels and supernatural phenomena. As you navigate the labyrinthine paths of the Crimson Carnival, be prepared to question the very nature of reality. Nothing is as it seems, and illusions merge with the tangible.

CHOOSE YOUR PATH — "Crimson Carnival" is not a passive experience. Your choices matter, and the consequences can be chilling. Engage in games of wit and strategy, where your decisions determine your fate. Will you embrace the darkness or resist its pull?

ENCOUNTER THE MACABRE — At the heart of the carnival lies a gallery of horrors. From eerie sideshow acts to grotesque exhibits, you'll come face to face with the bizarre and the grotesque. Witness the eerie contortions of the acrobats and the unsettling talents of the carnival's denizens.
