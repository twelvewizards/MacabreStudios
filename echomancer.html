Courage Required Sanity Optional

Dive into the harrowing depths of "Echomancer," a psychological horror game inspired by the haunting allure of real-life paranormal investigations. You are a paranormal investigator tasked with unearthing the dark secrets of an abandoned asylum. Your mission is clear: uncover the truth that lurks within these decaying walls, guided only by your wits and a unique device known as the "Echoceptor".

FEATURES:

A JOURNEY INTO THE PARANORMAL — Delve into an original, bone-chilling storyline that draws you into the tragic history and unsettling occurrences in an abandoned mental asylum.

A LABYRINTH OF HORROR — Navigate through a maze-like asylum filled with dark corridors, eerie cells, and nightmarish medical facilities. The setting is as much a character as any, its walls echoing with the remnants of tragedies long past.

THE ECHOCEPTER — Utilize an innovative handheld device that lets you interact with the echoes of the past. The Echoceptor is your key to solving mind-bending puzzles, collecting clues, and discovering the shocking events that befell the asylum's previous occupants.

In "Echomancer," your choices will haunt you, and your bravery will be your saving grace. How you face the supernatural and confront your own fears will shape your understanding of the grim reality—and perhaps even yourself. Prepare for a gripping, spine-tingling experience that marries the visceral elements of classic survival horror with fresh gameplay mechanics.
