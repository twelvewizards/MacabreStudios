Survival is just a puzzle away on this eternal train ride.

"The Locomotive" throws you into a spine-chilling journey aboard a never-ending, steam-powered train. The mystery begins with your arrival on this eerie locomotive — you have no recollection of how you got here. Your mission is clear: find the conductor at the front of the train and uncover the truth behind your inexplicable presence.

FEATURES:

PUZZLES OF PERIL — The train itself is a riddle, designed to challenge your intellect and bravery. Solve these enigmas to advance. Failure could mean perpetual captivity within this nightmarish locomotive.

DISCOVER THE CONDUCTOR — The conductor remains an enigma, their identity veiled in shadows. As you venture deeper into the heart of the train's malevolence, you will gradually unveil the truth about this enigmatic figure and the sinister purpose of the train.

HAUNTING DIVERSITY — Each carriage is a distorted reality, representing various time periods and dystopian futures. Explore these nightmarish settings while inching closer to your objective.

MORAL CROSSROADS — Your choices influence the grim destiny of the train and its occupants. Will you confront the malevolent forces head-on, or risk being consumed by them? The repercussions of your decisions are ever-present.

In "The Locomotive," the mystery begins with your inexplicable presence on this never-ending, haunted train. As you explore the train's labyrinthine carriages, you'll seek answers and strive to unveil the conductor's secrets. Can you survive the relentless terror, decipher the riddles of the locomotive, and uncover the truth behind your enigmatic journey, or will you be forever lost within its horrors? Your destiny hinges on every choice you make.
